Norman Bakes - Email Setup

This will register your email address with Formspree for free order notifications.

What happens when you click "Setup Email Notifications":
  • A test email will be sent to normanbakes38@gmail.com
  • Formspree will ask you to verify your email address
  • Once verified, all cake orders will be emailed to you automatically
  • This service is completely free (up to 50 emails/month)

Instructions:

  1. Click the button above
  2. Check your email (normanbakes38@gmail.com) for a verification email from Formspree
  3. Click the verification link in that email
  4. Your website will then automatically send order notifications to your email
